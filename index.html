Frequently Asked Questions

Your quick guide to common questions about accounts, setup, and features.

How do I reset my password?

Navigate to the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password," and enter your registered email. We will send a secure link to reset your credentials instantly.

What devices are supported?

AttendEase supports all major browsers on desktop and mobile apps Android only.

Is there a free trial period?

Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial for all new school accounts. No credit card required.

How do I add students?

Teachers can easily add students through the dashboard by importing a CSV file or adding them individually with their details.

Can I export attendance reports?

Absolutely! Export attendance data as PDF or Excel files for easy sharing and record-keeping.
